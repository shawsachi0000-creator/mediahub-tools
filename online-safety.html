How to Stay Safe Online

The internet is a valuable resource, but staying safe online is important. Strong passwords, two-factor authentication, and avoiding suspicious links can greatly improve your security.

Use Strong Passwords

Create unique passwords for every account and avoid using personal information.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Whenever possible, enable 2FA to add an extra layer of protection.

Be Careful with Downloads

Only download files from trusted websites and always scan them with antivirus software.

Keep Software Updated

Install updates regularly to protect your device from known security vulnerabilities.
